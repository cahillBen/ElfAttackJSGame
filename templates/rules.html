Tips

To play again just pick a game mode!

  • Use arrow keys to move
  • Press {{hit}} to use your sword
  • Press {{skipKey}} to skip a level
  • Press {{invincibleKey}} to become Invincibile
  • Press {{shoot}} to use your bow
  • Arrows aim towards an enemy, you can't control this
  • Click with your Mouse to summon a tornado

Features

  • Running Mini-Game
  • Fight Mechanics (Sword/Bow)
  • Pause Screen between levels
  • Obstacles (Fences/Walls)
  • Hearts Increase Health
  • Fight Multiple Enemies at once
  • Cheats (Invincibility/Skip Level)
  • Tense Fight Music
  • 5 Different Enemy Types
  • Blackout Levels (Reduced Visibility)
  • Moving Environment on Cave map
  • Special Powers (Summon Tornado/Teleport)
  • Powers have Cooldown Timers,Animations
  • 2 Game Modes (Story Mode/Endless Mode) in Single Page Application
  • Story mode - explains features, different enemies, explosive landmines
  • Endless mode - fight till you die, randomly generated levels
  • Leaderboard of Endless Mode scores
  • Change Game Controls in Settings
